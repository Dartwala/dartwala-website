Our Services

At Dartwala Express, we deliver more than just courier bookings — we deliver smarter shipping solutions designed to simplify, optimize, and accelerate your business growth.

Our Core Services

  • Multi-Courier Shipping Access — Access multiple courier partners under a single platform, choose the best-fit service for every shipment.
  • International Courier Services — Fast and reliable delivery to major countries worldwide, with customs and documentation support.
  • Part Truck Load (PTL) Services — Economical bulk shipping without booking an entire truck. Best for medium-volume goods across cities.
  • Full Truck Load (FTL) Services — Dedicated truck bookings for large and heavy shipments with point-to-point delivery.
  • Next Day Delivery Solutions — Critical shipments delivered within 24 hours in selected metro and major cities.
  • Same Day Delivery Solutions — Ultra-urgent intra-city deliveries completed the same day, wherever available.
  • Packers and Movers (Relocation Services) — Safe and insured shifting solutions for households, offices, and vehicles.
