What is PTL?

PTL (Part Truck Load) shipping is ideal for businesses that need to ship goods larger than parcel size but don’t require an entire truck. We combine your shipment with other compatible loads, helping reduce costs while maintaining efficient delivery.

Key Features of Our PTL Services

  • Cost Effective: Share transport cost with other consignors
  • Flexible Volume: 20kg to 2 tons of capacity
  • Hub-to-Hub & Door Delivery: Available based on route
  • Branded Partners: Shree Maruti, VRL, Gati, TCI and more
  • Digital Tracking & POD: Real-time visibility of movement

When Should You Choose PTL?

  • You have frequent shipments across states but not truckload volumes
  • Your delivery deadlines are flexible within 2–6 days
  • You want to reduce freight costs for medium-load volumes
  • Your shipment requires secure transit with minimal handling
