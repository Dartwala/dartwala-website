What is FTL?

FTL (Full Truck Load) shipping offers an exclusive transportation solution where one shipment occupies the full capacity of a truck. Ideal for large volumes, dedicated movement, and faster deliveries.

Why FTL with Dartwala?

  • Pan India Network: Trucks available across metro, tier-1, and rural zones
  • Direct Route Movement: No mid-transit hubs or consolidations
  • Variety of Trucks: LCV, HCV, Containers, Open Body & Custom Types
  • Real-Time Tracking: GPS-monitored fleet with driver coordination

Industries Benefiting from FTL

  • Automotive Manufacturing & Spare Movement
  • Bulk Raw Materials (Paper, Plastic, Pharma)
  • Plant-to-Depot Distribution (FMCG, Paints)
  • Wholesale Retail & E-commerce Fulfillment

Advantages of FTL

  • Faster delivery timelines with dedicated truck
  • Less handling reduces damage/loss risk
  • Option to seal and brand your shipment
  • Customized delivery slot booking
