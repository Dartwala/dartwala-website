What is Same Day Delivery?

Same Day Delivery is a premium express logistics service where shipments are delivered on the same day of pickup — typically within city or metro regions. Ideal for urgent, time-critical deliveries.

Where It Works

  • Intra-city: Same city pickup & drop (e.g., Mumbai to Mumbai)
  • Metro Corridors: High-speed lanes (e.g., Delhi-Gurgaon, Mumbai-Pune, Hyderabad-Secunderabad)
  • Customized urgent dispatches with bike, van or mini-truck

Top Use Cases

  • Legal documents, tenders, and contracts
  • Medical samples or health kits
  • E-commerce express orders
  • Machinery parts and business-critical components

Features of Our Same Day Delivery
